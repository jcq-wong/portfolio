Weather — live data visualization, p5.js
Weather
A live weather visualization for 29 cities around the world — from the Sahara to Svalbard. Temperature shifts the color of a large circle from blue to yellow. Wind spins a five-pointed star at proportional speed. Humidity fills an arc. Precipitation falls as particles. Click to cycle through cities.

Where's the corn? — data storytelling, p5.js
Where's the corn?
An interactive visualization exploring how corn derivatives — cornstarch, high fructose corn syrup, ethanol, and more — make their way into over 100 everyday products, from aspirin to rubber tires to drywall. Hover any product to see which derivatives it contains. Filter by derivative to see the network light up. And somewhere in the scene, there's a hidden ear of corn to find.
